About Us

Magnolia Band Directors

The Magnolia Band Directors (MBD) is a 501c Non-profit organization dedicated to supporting minority directors in Mississippi. The association's focus is to create a community of support for directors; cultivate concert, clinic, and conference opportunities for the professional development of directors; and recognize and acknowledge directors who have contributed to molding lives through the art of music in Mississippi.

The main focus areas include maintaining a director database to aid in networking opportunities for directors; sponsoring professional enhancement opportunities such as honor concert clinics and performances and conferences to support music programs in Mississippi; Sponsoring events such as banquets, luncheons, dinners, concerts, and other programs to recognize and acknowledge minority directors who have made notable contributions through the art of music in Mississippi; Encouraging and supporting directors to build music programs that include:

Teaching all aspects of music, including, but not limited to, concert, jazz, ensemble, percussion, marching bands, and other ensembles;

Teaching fundamentals, all scales, techniques, and literature to prepare students to participate in music at elementary, middle school, high school, and college levels;

Teaching musicianship, character, citizenship, and other qualities;

Teaching through performance opportunities that evaluate concert, marching, and ensemble groups.
